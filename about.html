About Erasmus Student Network (ESN)

Erasmus Student Network (ESN) is one of the biggest interdisciplinary student associations in Europe, founded in 1989 for supporting and developing student exchange. We are present in more than 1000 Higher Education Institutions from 42 countries. The network is constantly developing and expanding.

Hey Buddy, Need a Buddy?

The Buddy System is a program designed to connect international students with local students who act as their "buddies." It's the perfect way to make new friends, adapt to a new environment, and experience cultural exchange while feeling supported.

Whether you're looking for guidance, help settling in, or someone to explore your new surroundings with, the Buddy System is here for you.

What is a Buddy?

Your "buddy" is a local student who can help you navigate life in a new country, from understanding the culture and language to finding essential services like banks, grocery stores, and transportation. They may even invite you to local events and introduce you to their social circles.

Cultural Exchange

The Buddy System is more than practical assistance. It's an opportunity for cultural exchange, fostering meaningful connections, and learning from one another's experiences. Both you and your buddy will gain a broader perspective on life and culture.

How It Works

Sign up through this form with your preferences and interests. You'll be matched with a local student based on shared hobbies, academic background, or personal preferences. Once paired, you'll start your journey together.

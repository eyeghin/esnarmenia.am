ESN Armenia Policies

We stand by the principles of inclusivity, transparency, and sustainability to support student mobility.

Key Policies

Inclusivity

Ensuring equal opportunities for all students to experience cultural exchange.

Sustainability

Encouraging environmentally friendly practices in all our activities.

Transparency

Maintaining open and honest communication at every level.

Our Principles

  • Support the social and practical integration of international students.
  • Provide relevant information about exchange programs and ESN resources.
  • Encourage returning students to share their experiences and enrich our community.
  • Advocate for student needs and expectations on local, national, and international platforms.

ESN is an independent, non-partisan, non-political, and non-profit international association operating under Belgian law. Supported by Erasmus+ and the Council of Europe.

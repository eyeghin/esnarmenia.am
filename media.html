ESN’s General Assembly gathers in Greece

From November 21st to the 24th, 2024, the Erasmus Student Network (ESN) held its Autumn General Assembly in Thessaloniki, bringing together representatives from across Europe to shape the future of the organisation.

The Assembly discussed updates on ESN's new Long-term Strategy, approved ESN Khazar as a Candidate Section, and celebrated the 20th anniversary of the ESNcard. The Assembly emphasized inclusivity, transparency, and collaboration.

Volunteers engaged in thought-provoking discussions on improving internal communication, fostering cultural exchange, and supporting international mobility.

The event was made possible by the collaboration of ESN Thessaloniki and the universities in the city, highlighting the impact of student associations.
